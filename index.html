Hey Folks!

  • This is a different version of BlackJack modified with dice.
  • The game starts by entering name of 2 players.
  • Player 1 starts first by pressing on roll dice button.
  • Whatever the dice rolls get's added into the current score.
  • Player loses the current score if dice rolls 1,
    or the player can hold the current Score in total score by pressing hold button.
  • If the player 1 chooses to hold or dice rolls 1 the player switches and Vice-Versa
  • The first player to reach 20 points wins
